{# Why two engines #}

// choose your engine

Liquid or Metal?

Both are serverless. Both scale to zero. Pick the one that fits your workload.

Liquid

  • Wasm sandbox — no kernel, no filesystem
  • Sub-millisecond cold starts
  • Best for: APIs, webhooks, data transforms
  • Target: wasm32-wasip1

Metal

  • Real Linux VM — full kernel, filesystem, networking
  • <15ms cold starts via snapshot restore
  • Best for: database drivers, FFI, syscalls, native deps
  • Target: x86_64-unknown-linux-musl
